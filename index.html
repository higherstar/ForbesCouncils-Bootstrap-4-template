As a member of the council benefits include...

Absolutely FREE

As an invitation-only advisory group, members do not pay anything and qualify to participate in the council benefits program. The benefits include private jet membership access, staffing services, event tickets, Mercedes S-class transfers and an anonymous group chat to get help with some of the most sophisticated software development-related issues. The council is designed to truly be the best in class for its group members.

Private Jet Discounted Access

Access to PrivateJet plan at the highest tier. Get complimentary access to the best pricing in the private aviation sector. You can use it for your personal or business purposes. Up to $50K per year per member.* Prices are hugely discounted over the market rates. You can find real-time retail pricing here: flyxo.com

Free Tickets to Annual Gala

Each year SWAG will host an event in beautiful La Jolla, California. Members are allowed to bring one guest at no charge and will receive tickets to attend one of the greatest classic and modern car shows in the US (https://www.lajollaconcours.com/) Oh, did we mention the event is right by the water with breathtaking views? Exactly…

Get Answers to tough Questions

An anonymous moderated platform where you can post your most complex software development questions and have your peers anonymously give advice and feedback. This allows you to seek 3rd party expert opinions that can help you navigate some your largest challenges, without anyone knowing who is asking. We all need a little help, and this platform offers a great way to expand on ideas and experiences when dealing with issues.

FREE Candidate sourcing

Through our partner channels, get one technical position filled at no cost for as long as you are a member. No strings attached ($25K value). This means software developers across pretty much any tech stack. So if it comes time where you need to source talent, your member status offers this great perk at no cost.

Moderated Content

All the messaging forums are moderated so only relevant content makes it to your inbox. We understand you get tons of emails. We do our part to only send communication that is relevant to you. Plus you can turn it off and on at any time. This helps ensure the integrity and quality of the forums. (Coming June 2020)

National Ferrari Events

Each quarter a draw will be held across the membership for a Ferrari drive day to a race closest to your residing city, for you and a guest to experience the thrill of driving an engineering masterpiece. You will need a valid driver’s license and attend a short 15-minute class onsite prior to driving.

Vetted Community with Input

Membership is accepted based on a set of criteria that must be met before a member is to be invited and accepted. This helps keep the conversations and connections interesting and fruitful. Plus members decide what the criteria will be each year, so the group agenda is aligned with the changing times and technologies.

Mercedes S Class Transfer

When you visit America’s finest city, you will receive complimentary one-way pick up and drop-off for you and a guest in an S Class within a 20 mile radius. This ride comes complete with reclining rear seats and sunshades on all the back windows. Arrive in style and yet another thank you for being an active contributor to the SWAG council.

2019-2020 Invitation Criteria

This is the criteria that must be met in order to receive an invitation into the advisory group. The criteria might change from year to year, but all existing members will be grandfathered into the changes.
